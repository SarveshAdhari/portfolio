Projects

News-App

NewsApp is a ReactJs web application that brings you the latest news. The main page showcases general news, while the navbar offers categorized links to topics like sports, entertainment, health, and more. Each category displays news specific to its topic. Powered by the newsdata.io API, NewsApp ensures access to reliable and up-to-date news content. Stay informed and explore the world of news through this user-friendly web application.

Recipe-App

Recipe Finder is a ReactJs web app where you can explore a wide variety of recipes. With a search box in the navbar, finding your favorite recipes is a breeze. Each recipe includes detailed ingredient lists and step-by-step instructions. Powered by the Spoonacular Recipe API, it offers a diverse culinary experience. This application is your gateway to discovering new flavors and mastering the art of cooking.Join us on this flavorful journey of discovery and delight..

Weather-App

The Weather Tracker is a React application that displays the temperature of any place. With a search box, you can easily look up the weather for your desired location. Utilizing the weatherapi, this app provides accurate and real-time temperature information. Stay informed about the weather conditions of any place, all within a user-friendly interface. Experience the convenience of the Weather Tracker as it keeps you updated on the temperature wherever you go.
